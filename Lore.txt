	Mobs
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

	[Four legged moss]
As long as left alone the four legged moss is peaceful. It wanders the forests eating mushroom. If disturbed by eating however;
it will defend itself.

	[Grubler]
The most common race in the forest world. It lives on rescources the forest
has to give. When the forest endured a dry period the treants didn't get enough water.
The Grublers helped them to survive by giving them water. In return the treants promised wood
as a building resource for the Grublers.

	[Rebos]
After thousands of years on the bottom of the sea learning about life the Rebos has figured out the
key to eternal life. For each year it grows more powerful and the only way of death is by combat.

	[Phab]
This mysterial creature knows how to travel in time. It is constantly learning about life by studiyng the past
and future.
